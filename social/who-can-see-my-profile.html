Anybody who knows your username can see your profile by visiting either http://preserve.io/user/USERNAME or http://preserve.io/u/USERNAME

We currently don't have a published list of usernames on the site, so unless you let people know that your username is puppylover69, there's a good chance nobody will stumble across your profile.

The only way that somebody could get to your profile without knowing your Preserve username is the off chance that they guess that you've got a Preserve account, and know that you use the same username across all services.

NOTE: There are currently NO profile privacy settings, however it is planned. When they are in place, this page will be updated.
