Why we built Preserve

Preserve is a designed to help you keep your bookmarks safe. Created by a few friends who are tired of hitting 404 pages, we're creating the best bookmarking tool in the world.

How it Works

When you bookmark a page using Preserve, we download a copy of the page, analyze it, and then will index it in our search engine. When you come back to your bookmark, you can view the original site or the Preserved copy, which is the copy of the page we saved when you originally bookmarked it.
